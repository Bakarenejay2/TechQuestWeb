About the Game

TechQUEST is an immersive 3D educational game designed to enhance the learning experience for students taking the 'Living in the IT Era' course at National University - Manila, Philippines. With interactive challenges and gamified lessons, TechQUEST helps students grasp complex IT concepts in an engaging and intuitive way.
